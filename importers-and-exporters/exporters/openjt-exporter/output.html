Output

Scenes​

The nodes present in your scene should generally be exported whilst maintaining their tree structure. To load them back into Visionary Render to confirm their structure, open a new scene and import the exported file using the accompanying Open JT importer. The root assembly of the export should match its filename.

warning

The tree structure when exported and re-imported may not always exactly match the original. For instance, due to differences in how Visionary Render and JT store data, extra levels of assemblies can sometimes be generated.

Libraries​

Any geometry, texture or material nodes that were exported will be added to the Libraries tree. The Library is named after the filename of the export (minus the extension).
