# 2026.2

Thank you for downloading GeoVisionary 2026.2. This release permits easier management of GeoVisionary installs, URLs in borehole data, and improved labels in shapefiles.

## What's New in This Release?

### Silent Installs

We have added support for silent installs to facilitate easier management of GeoVisionary installs across your organisation. This permits administrators to quickly install, update and remove GeoVisionary via central management systems such as inTune, MECM, and SCCM. 

### URLs in Borehole Data

Borehole data often contains links to websites with additional information on them, such as scanned borehole logs and photographs. To prevent you from having to manually enter these in your browser, we have added support for URL hyperlinks in borehole data, as per URLs in Shapefile data.

### Label Size in GVStyles

To prevent labels in shapefiles from merging together when flying away from them, we have added a minimum/maximum scale, offset property (Z offset), and depth test toggle to the Billboard metanode.
